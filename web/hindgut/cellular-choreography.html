Sheets into Tubes — Cellular Choreography / Seed

Problem → Uncoordinated luminal cuticle deposition warps apices & tube caliber • Objective → Couple secretion to cell shape to set & hold diameter • Link → Patterned chitin deposition + crosslinking (flexible corset) with mechanosensitive feedback to apical actomyosin

Core intent: A patterned, crosslinked luminal “corset,” read through apical mechanosensors to tune actomyosin, couples secretion to shape—stabilizing diameter and preventing apex warping.

  • Uniform caliber; fluctuations damp out

Load‑Bearing Test

  1. Identify where the loop sets diameter vs where it holds it; explain back.
  2. Predict before reveal for three toggles: (i) ring gap → localized scallop/dent with confined spread; (ii) global crosslink stiffening → over‑constriction with stepwise caliber plateaus; (iii) reduced mechanosensitivity → slow, drifting caliber with poor recovery.
  3. Bonus: propose independent readouts (tension banding aligns with rings; pressure alone cannot reproduce banded tension).
