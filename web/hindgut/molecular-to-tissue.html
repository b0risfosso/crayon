Molecular-to-Tissue Chirality Cascade — Hindgut Rotation / Seed

Problem → PCP vectors and chiral cues appear orthogonal, obscuring integration • Objective → Unify PCP orientation with chiral vorticity • Link → Coupled-field model: PCP sets alignment, chirality sets vorticity

Core intent: Test whether a coupled-field rule (PCP provides alignment frame; chirality provides signed vorticity) predicts rotation direction and magnitude under combined perturbations.

Load-Bearing Test

Experts click scenarios, adjust κ and PCP-weight, and see that only the projection rule reproduces reversals and stalls. Validation if they can map scenarios to known outcomes and propose next experiments.

First Eyes

  • Drosophila asymmetry labs (Myo1D/Myo1C)
  • PCP signaling experts (Frizzled/Stan, Fat/Ds)
  • Biophysical modelers of cortical flows
  • Imaging scientists (PIV/vorticity maps)
  • Skeptics arguing PCP and chirality independence

Measurement & Signals

Sliders (1–5)

  • Did projection (cosθ) coupling feel sufficient to unify PCP and chirality?
  • Did the alternative models (independent, chirality-only) fail at least one scenario?
  • Would you trust this coupled-field rule to design a perturbation?

Observer Checklist

  • Viewer narrates back how PCP angle gates torque
  • Viewer predicts double-perturbation stall vs reversal
  • Viewer explains why chirality flips direction while PCP rotation stalls

Validation Signals

  • ≥70% select 4–5 on “projection coupling convincing”
  • ≥60% correctly predict outcomes of PCP-90° and double perturbation
  • ≥50% propose a concrete next experiment (e.g., rotate Ds gradient with Myo1D knockdown)

Why This is a Box of Dirt

Minimal: mocked overlays and a single coupling dial with pre-scripted outcomes. Disposable: discard if projection idea fails. Growth-inviting: later ingest real PCP vectors and PIV flows, compare fits, and generalize. Alternative-hypothesis toggle seeds decisive experiments, turning the sketch into a roadmap.
