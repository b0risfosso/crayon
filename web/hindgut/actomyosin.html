Actomyosin Choreography and Tube Shaping — Hindgut Morphogenesis / Seed

Problem → Uniform E-cadherin turnover under load causes tears • Objective → Stabilize high-tension interfaces while patterning line tension • Link → Tension-gated endocytic switch

Core intent: A tension-gated E-cadherin turnover rule stabilizes stressed edges and accelerates removal on low-tension edges, preventing junctional failure and guiding anisotropic remodeling.

Panel 5 — Perturbation Toggles

  • Raise load → integrity maintained until threshold
  • Invert threshold → misassigned stabilization, buckling

Panel 6 — Predictive Signatures

  • FRAP half-time increases with tension only with switch active
  • Pit lifetimes ↑ on high-tension, frequency ↑ on low-tension
  • Under load, T1 align with low-tension map; tear frequency drops
  • Opto-increasing tension stabilizes cadherin; relaxation accelerates removal
