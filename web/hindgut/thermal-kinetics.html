Thermal Kinetics vs Chiral Bias — Hindgut Rotation / Seed

Problem → Arrhenius slopes of ω(T) conflate motor, turnover, and drag • Objective → Deconvolve activation energies • Link → ω(T) ∝ [k_myo(T)·k_turnover(T)]/η(T) with perturbation reweighting

Core intent: Test whether a composite Arrhenius model with separable activation energies for motor torque, turnover, and viscous drag can explain temperature dependence of hindgut rotation and chirality.

Why This is a Box of Dirt
