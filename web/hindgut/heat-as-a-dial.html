Heat as a Dial on Force & Material State — Hindgut / Seed

Problem → Acute heat pulses fluidize cortex & destabilize actomyosin → erratic deformations, lost fidelity • Objective → Buffer mechanics against thermal spikes without blunting force • Link → Precondition to raise Hsp70/Hsp90; preserve contractility & viscosity setpoints; verify via laser‑recoil & AFM modulus across shocks

Core intent: Preconditioning that elevates Hsp70/Hsp90 can buffer cortical material state so acute heat spikes do not shift contractile force or effective viscosity enough to derail hindgut morphogenesis.
