Planar Cell Chirality as a Field — Hindgut Twist / Seed

Problem → Roles of Fz/Dsh vs Fat/Ds/Dachs in setting chirality azimuth are unresolved • Objective → Causally rotate/realign the planar‑chirality field to identify which module drags the rotation axis • Link → Tilt Ds/Dachs slopes or acutely relocalize Dsh and read out azimuthal deflection

Core intent: Forcibly rotate one PCP module while holding the other fixed; the hindgut’s chirality azimuth (and twist axis) should follow the module that truly sets planar chirality.

Why This is a Box of Dirt

Disposable, pre‑scripted panels with two rotatable fields and a single readout (axis deflection with latency). No solver or data. Easy to swap in real reporter maps, boundary conditions, or quantitative couplings once the decisive manipulation is agreed upon.
