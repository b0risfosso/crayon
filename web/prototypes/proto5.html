Wake the Dormant Muscle — Satellite‑Cell Niche Reset

A (Problem): Aging stiffens the satellite‑cell niche and drives senescence, stalling repair and depleting progenitors.
B (Objective): A rejuvenated, self‑renewing satellite‑cell pool that rapidly regenerates muscle after injury.
Link: Injectable, soft laminin‑rich hydrogel with timed pulses (senolytics, Notch agonist, brief OSK mRNA) and mechano‑cues that reset YAP/TAZ.

Core intent: Softening an aged niche and delivering brief, timed cues can clear senescence, restore youthful mechanosignaling, and yield fast repair with a replenished Pax7+ reserve.

Metrics Legend (plain language)

  • Senescence index: fewer damaged, non‑dividing cells.
  • Notch tone: readiness of stem cells to self‑renew.
  • YAP/TAZ balance: youthful mechanosensing (not stuck nuclear).
  • Pax7+ pool: reserve of satellite cells available for repair.
  • Regeneration score: composite of early repair markers.
  • Fibrosis risk: scarring tendency during healing.
  • Force output: recovered contractile strength.
  • Survival: proportion of reattached/engrafted cells that persist.
